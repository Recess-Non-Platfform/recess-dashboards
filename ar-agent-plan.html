📧 Follow-Up Type Matrix

Each invoice gets classified into one of these follow-up types based on urgency and balance:

🟢 Pre-Due Reminder — 7 days before due 🟡 Gentle Follow-Up — 1–14 days overdue
🟠 Firm Follow-Up — 15–30 days overdue, >$1K 🔴 Escalation — 31–60 days overdue, >$1K
🔴 Final Notice — 60+ days overdue, >$1K

Tiered amount rule: Pre-due and gentle follow-ups keep it conversational (no dollar amounts). Firm, escalation, and final notice emails include invoice numbers AND amounts — AP departments need specifics to action payment.

Status overrides: Accounts can be flagged as dispute, payment_plan, write_off, or do_not_contact to suppress or adjust follow-ups.

⚠️ Risks & Mitigations

HIGH — Missing HubSpot Links

70% of AR by $ has no email context

Mitigation: Phase 0 checklist above. Agent works in degraded mode (template-only drafts) for unlinked accounts. Clear flag in digest: "⚠️ No HubSpot link — template-only draft"

HIGH — Draft Quality Variance

Bad drafts erode trust faster than good ones build it

Mitigation: All drafts reviewed by Deuce for first 2 weeks. Professional default tone (no voice matching v1). Post-generation validation catches leaked amounts/terms.

MEDIUM — Stale Data

Fivetran sync can lag 6-24h

Mitigation: Every digest shows "Data as of" timestamp. If >24h stale, digest shows ⚠️ warning. Payment detected in QBO but not yet synced = potential for embarrassing follow-up on paid invoice.

MEDIUM — Alert Fatigue

Too many items = team ignores everything

Mitigation: Cap at 15 items per digest. Top 10 by urgency + 5 rotating accounts that haven't surfaced recently. Penny remainders (<$10) auto-filtered.

MEDIUM — Dispute/Payment Plan Accounts

Sending collection emails to accounts with known disputes

Mitigation: Status override field per customer (dispute, payment_plan, write_off, do_not_contact). Pre-populated during Phase 0 from Deuce's knowledge. Checked before every draft.

MEDIUM — Schedule Gaps

Invoice due Tue/Thu/Sat with no digest that day

Mitigation: "LAST CHANCE" flag on prior digest for invoices due next day. Ad-hoc Slack alert for high-value (>$10K) invoices within 48h regardless of schedule.
