💡 The Vision

Turn customer & sponsor news monitoring into an interactive sales weapon. Instead of a passive news feed, the Sales Intelligence Engine:

  • Scans news about your sponsors (buyers) and supply partners daily
  • Enriches every item with full HubSpot context — deals, contacts, owners, last activity
  • Posts formatted digests to dedicated Slack channels with @-mentions to deal owners
  • Flags stale deals (7+ days no contact) and cold contacts (10+ days) with open HubSpot tasks
  • Lets reps reply "draft email to [name]" and get a personalized outreach email using full activity history
  • Matches each rep's writing voice from their prior sent emails
  • Detects job changes and company leadership changes as outreach triggers

Goal: Give your sales team a reason to ping with a timely, personalized email — every week.
