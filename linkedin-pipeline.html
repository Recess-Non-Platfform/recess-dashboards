Stages 4 & 5

Draft Selection & Generation

Drafts are distributed by pillar weight, then generated using Deuce's voice profile with strict quality gates.

✂️ Selection Logic

  • 1. Allocate draft slots per pillar based on weights
  • 2. If pillar has 0 slots → content NEVER becomes a draft
  • 3. Select highest-scored item per pillar
  • 4. Fill shortfall from highest-scored remaining items

🚀 Proposed Improvement

AI-Powered Content Analyzer

Replace keyword matching with an AI analyzer that reads content and assigns pillars based on what's actually in it.

❌ Current Flow (Broken)

  • 1 Content comes in from Airtable
  • 2 Check source type (Fireflies? RSS?)
  • 3 Assign pillar by keyword/source

  • 5 Score items

✅ Proposed Flow (AI Analyzer)

  • 1 Content comes in from Airtable
  • 2 AI reads actual content
  • 3 Assigns pillar based on substance
  • 4 Extracts key insights & quotes
  • 5 Pre-filters internal meetings
