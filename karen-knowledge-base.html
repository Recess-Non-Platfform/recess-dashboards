📋 Category 1: PO / Invoice Rejection 56 pairs — 47%

The #1 AR blocker. Nearly half of all Q&A pairs involve PO numbers, invoice rejections, and resubmissions. Karen MUST check deal PO properties before drafting any follow-up.

📥 Common Incoming Patterns

  • "Could you share the PO number for this?"
  • "Invoices rejected with error [X]"
  • "Can you resubmit with the correct PO?"
  • "Please include PO on all invoices"

📤 Standard Recess Responses

  • Provide PO number from deal property
  • Reattach invoices with corrections
  • Confirm PO process (one per contract vs one per invoice)
  • Loop in accounting for resubmission
🤖 Karen Auto-Draft: Pull PO number from deal, attach invoice reference, ask what format they need.

💰 Category 2: Payment Status 13 pairs

📥 Common Incoming Patterns

  • "Payment vouchers attached, can you confirm receipt?"
  • "Check was mailed on [date]"
  • "Processing payment this week"

📤 Standard Recess Responses

  • Confirm receipt or flag non-receipt
  • Request tracking info for checks
  • Thank for payment confirmation
🤖 Karen Auto-Draft: Check QBO for payment match, confirm or flag discrepancy.

📄 Category 3: W9 / Vendor Setup 6 pairs

📥 Common Incoming Patterns

  • "Can you send over your W-9?"
  • "Need W9 and Payment Method Forms"
  • "What's your vendor number?"

📤 Standard Recess Responses

  • Attach W9 (standard doc)
  • Provide vendor number if known
  • Loop in accounting for payment method forms
🤖 Karen Auto-Draft: Attach W9 from standard location, note vendor number if available.

🏦 Category 4: Remittance / ACH Info 3 pairs

📥 Common Incoming Patterns

  • "Can you resend wire info?"
  • "What account should we pay to?"

📤 Standard Recess Responses

  • Send wire/ACH details
  • Reference notice of assignment if applicable
🤖 Karen Auto-Draft: Provide standard remittance details from config. ⚠ ALWAYS requires human approval — even in Phase 5.

⚖️ Category 5: Credit / Adjustment 0 pairs — Always Human

Rule: ALWAYS route to human. Karen never auto-responds to credit or adjustment requests — no exceptions, regardless of automation phase.

Credit requests involve financial decisions that require human judgment. No historical Q&A pairs exist because these were always handled by Deuce or accounting directly.
